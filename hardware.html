MaLa Hardware

MaLa Hardware enables you to connect an LCD (from 1×16 up to 4×40 characters) and up to 64 LEDs to your cabinet. The LCD can display various information from games and game lists, and the LEDs can be used to light your buttons — controlled by game information, as shown in the pictures above. MaLa Hardware connects via USB to your computer.

Available Configurations

Two board versions are available. One or two boards can be connected in these combinations:

  • LCD + 16 LEDs (1 board)
  • 32 LEDs (1 board)
  • LCD + 48 LEDs (2 boards)
  • 64 LEDs (2 boards)

Pushbuttons

The MaLa Hardware board supports standard arcade pushbuttons. Each button LED can be individually controlled based on game data, creating a dynamic lit control panel experience.
