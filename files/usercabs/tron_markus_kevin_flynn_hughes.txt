Tron

Markus (Kevin Flynn) Hughes


This is my retro cab dedicated to the 
classic movie/Arcade cab Tron.  Made 
from pressed board, plexiglas, 
florescent paint, black lights, and lots 
of geeking out.  Speakers are installed 
inside along with the power/volume 
switch wired into the front of the cab.  
Currently, I am not running MALA yet but 
that\'s my next trick.  I use an older 
PC, win xp Home Ed. sp2.  
Let there be Tron...
